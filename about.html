About Me

Who am I?

BIO

Hey there my name is Herman, I am 25 years old and I am Student at the University of Bremen in Germany, where I study Digital Media. I have always enjoyed video games ever since I was young and because of that I decided to study Digital Media so I could develop my own games someday!

Current Semester

I am in Finland!

This semester was very special for me, as I spent an entire semester in the lovely country of Finland! In total I just spent a little over 4 months here in Finland from August 25th to December 26th. I really enjoyed the cold weather and all the beautiful snow throughout the beginning of winter. Sadly I didn't meet to many new people and alot of my courses were online self study courses, which meant that I didn't really go to the University all that often. But aside from that it was awesome!
